About Wallet Nakama

Wallet Nakama is your friendly companion for smarter money management. Inspired by the word "Nakama" — meaning trusted friends and companions — Wallet Nakama helps you stay on top of your finances without the stress or complexity of traditional budgeting apps.

Track your spending, set category budgets, and visualise where your money goes — all in one clean, simple dashboard. Your data is saved per account.

What We Offer

📊

Visual Insights

Live pie charts and progress bars help you visualise your spending distribution at a glance.

💱

Multi-Currency

Track income and expenses in NGN, USD, or GBP with real-time conversion throughout the app.

🎯

Budget Goals

Set monthly targets per category and track how close you are to staying within budget.

🔒

Secure & Private

Each user's data is isolated. Your transactions and goals are accessible only from your account.

📅

Budget Calculator

Plan your monthly income and expenses, see a live summary, and save it as real transactions.

📱

Fully Responsive

Works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Your finances, anywhere you go.
